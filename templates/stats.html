Privacy policy

  • Your IP address is publicly logged.
  • This includes every action, including views as well as voting.
  • You can view the log for any given webm by hovering over the square in the top right-hand corner of the screen.
  • You can mask your IP by filling in the "who are you?" form.
  • The form will change subsequent log entries, but won't change anything that's already stored.
  • We will usually mask the IP address with some form of real-world identifer, such as name or nickname.
  • If you don't want this to happen, or want your identifier removed, contact the sysadmin at info@ this website, or via another method.
  • In addition, we use Google Analytics.
  • In the event of an error in the website, your details will be sent to a third-party aggregator which we use to keep track of problems.

Abuse policy

  • Webms are pulled from another (technically work-safe) social media website without vetting.
  • If you see something terrible, hit "report" where possible, which will instantly render the webm inaccessible.
  • This can usually be done by clicking "bad" then "report" – you may want to open the link in a new tab before hitting "bad".
  • Report flagrant abuse, or copyright queries, to abuse@fluv.net.

Development

  • Patches and issues welcome: https://github.com/zuzak/yakc
  • API available at http://api.webm.website/stats.json
